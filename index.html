Our Work Process

  • 01

    Estimations

    Our AI calculates how much space will be necessary to harvest our crops

  • 02

    Laser Leveling

    Our AV precisely laser levels the area, based on previous calculations from our AI

  • 03

    Drones

    Drones sow our desired crop and imprint non-invasive sensors on the plant.

  • 04

    Sensors

    Sensor data is uploaded into the cloud, providing us with real-time data

  • 05

    Drip Irrigation

    Finally, our AI adjusts pump pressure based on sensor data to maximize yield
